PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMMAN NIDHI (PM-KISAN)

Ministry: Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
Government of India

SCHEME OVERVIEW:
PM-KISAN is a Central Sector Scheme launched on 1st December 2018 to provide income support to all landholding farmers' families across the country.

OBJECTIVE:
To supplement the financial needs of the farmers in procuring various inputs to ensure proper crop health and appropriate yields.

BENEFITS:
- Financial benefit of Rs. 6,000 per year
- Amount paid in three equal installments of Rs. 2,000 each
- Direct transfer to bank accounts of beneficiaries
- Payment every four months

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
1. All landholding farmers' families
2. Family defined as husband, wife and minor children
3. Applicable to both small and marginal farmers
4. Land ownership is mandatory

EXCLUSION CATEGORIES (Not Eligible):
- Institutional land holders
- Farmer families holding constitutional posts
- Serving or retired officers and employees of Central/State Government
- All superannuated/retired pensioners with monthly pension of Rs. 10,000 or more
- Income Tax payers in last assessment year
- Professionals like doctors, engineers, lawyers, chartered accountants

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
1. Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
2. Land ownership documents (Khata/Khatauni)
3. Bank account details with IFSC code
4. Mobile number linked to Aadhaar

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Step 1: Visit PM-KISAN portal (pmkisan.gov.in) or nearest Common Service Center (CSC)
Step 2: Click on "Farmers Corner" and select "New Farmer Registration"
Step 3: Enter Aadhaar number and mobile number
Step 4: Fill in personal details, bank account information
Step 5: Upload land records (Khata/Khatauni)
Step 6: Submit application
Step 7: Note down registration number for future reference
Step 8: Verification by local revenue officials
Step 9: Approval and first installment within 2-3 months

OFFICIAL WEBSITE: https://pmkisan.gov.in

HELPLINE: 155261 / 011-24300606

TARGET BENEFICIARIES:
- Small farmers (landholding up to 2 hectares)
- Marginal farmers
- All landholding farmers across India
- Rural and semi-urban agricultural families

STATE COVERAGE: All states and Union Territories of India

IMPLEMENTATION: Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode through Aadhaar-linked bank accounts
